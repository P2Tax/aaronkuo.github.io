About

Tax technology, data, and practical implementation.

I work at the intersection of tax, data, technology, and operating model design, with a focus on turning complex regulatory requirements into systems and workflows that teams can actually run.

Focused on building clearer tax delivery models across Pillar Two, tax data, reporting workflows, and AI-enabled ways of working.

Professional positioning

Making complex tax work more usable.

My work focuses on how tax rules become repeatable delivery: data requirements, calculation logic, source mapping, review workflows, stakeholder ownership, and the technology choices that sit around them.

I am especially interested in the practical middle layer between technical interpretation and operational execution, where good design can reduce ambiguity and make reporting more scalable.

Personal lens

Curious about how people think, build, and document.

Outside the immediate work of tax delivery, I spend time exploring how AI changes the way professionals write, reason, organise knowledge, and turn expertise into reusable systems.

That lens shapes how I approach this site as well: clear explanations, practical examples, and a bias toward making complicated topics easier to work with.
