General introduction

A clear starting point for understanding OECD Pillar Two and the global minimum tax.

What it covers

The book introduces the foundations, mechanics, calculations, safe harbours, filing concepts, and practical context behind Pillar Two.

It is designed for readers who want a plain-English overview of how the regime works before going deeper into technical guidance, data, reporting, or operating model design.

Core Themes

  • FoundationsBackground, policy intent, scope, and the core architecture of the rules.
  • MechanicsCharging rules, calculations, ETR analysis, adjustments, and safe harbour concepts.
  • ImplementationData readiness, workflow design, controls, and the practical burden of compliance.
