Designing a Pillar Two Compliance Model Across 100+ Entities

Large multinational group · Pillar Two implementation · 100+ entities

Led the design and delivery of an end-to-end Pillar Two implementation, turning complex rule requirements into a workable compliance model across data, process, and reporting.

Challenge

The core issue was not technical interpretation alone. Data sat across multiple sources, ownership was fragmented, and the compliance process needed to work at scale across jurisdictions.

What I led

Directed the implementation approach across requirements, data logic, workflow design, and stakeholder coordination, including covered tax, deferred tax, ETR logic, and the broader operating structure.

Outcome

Helped create a more usable compliance model with clearer inputs, stronger workflow discipline, and a practical path to scaled Pillar Two reporting.
